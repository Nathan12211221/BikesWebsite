Coyote Clearwater Womens 26in 18 Speed Alloy Mtb Mountain Bike

The Coyote Clearwater Womens 26in 18 Speed Alloy MTB Mountain Bike is part of the Coyote mountain bike range, a breed apart, these bikes have been developed to combine both product performance, stylish designs and exceptional value. The Clearwater comes with a unique ladies lightweight aluminium 26 frame and Zoom premium branded front suspension with alloy double wall 36 hole black rims with front quick release hub. Alloy V brakes with Shimano 3-finger levers for strong stopping power. Shimano 21 speed gears with EZ-Fire shifters offer reliable gear changing.

  • Frame: Alloy

  • Cassette/Freewheel: 14-28 Tooth Freewheel

  • Brakes: Alloy V

  • Wheels: 26"" Alloy 36 Hole Black Rims

  • Colour: Grey

  • Frame Sizes: 15in, 17in, 19in
